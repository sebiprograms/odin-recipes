Pepperoni Pizza

Finally a Good Simple Recipe

Don't skip this to die for recipe! All it takes is a few simple steps and you'll thank me later you listened. Lets get started.

Ingredients

  • Dough (store bought or homemade)
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Italian Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425
  2. Take premade/homemade dough and shape as needed onto baking sheet.
  3. Apply tomatoe sauce as needed tell evenly covered.
  4. Add cheese tell sauce is fully covered.
  5. Top with pepperoni
  6. Bake for 8-11 minutes at 425

Enjoy!
