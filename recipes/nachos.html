Insane Nachos

Insanity Level Nachos

After this recipe there's no going back to dipping chips in bean dip. Lets jump right in and learn how to make Insane Nachos!

Ingredients

  • Corn chips
  • Queso
  • Cheese
  • Your choice of meat
  • Sour cream
  • Canned refried beans
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400.
  2. Put base layer of chips onto baking sheet. (don't overlap chips)
  3. Spread cheese and queso, beans onto first chip layer. Then top with another layer repeat as many times as you like.
  4. Top final layer with mounds of cheese bake at 400 for 5mins
  5. Garnish with cilantro

Enjoy!
